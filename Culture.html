78th Miss Silliman


The 78th Miss Silliman pageant was held on August 16, 2024 5: 00 pm at Henry's Resort - Bamboo Pavilion, showcasing the talents, intelligence, and beauty of Silliman University students. The event was a celebration of confidence, grace, and advocacy as the contestants presented their platforms and performances to a large audience. As a spectator, I observed the creativity and effort behind the event, from the production to the contestants' presentations. The pageant aimed to highlight individuality while promoting meaningful causes, making it more than just a competition.


Attending the pageant allowed me to reflect on the importance of self-confidence and using platforms to create positive change. I expected a traditional beauty competition, but I was pleasantly surprised by the focus on advocacy and substance, which made me feel inspired. A memorable moment was Q and A portion, which stood out because it showcased the contestants' depth and unique perspectives. The most fulfilling aspect was witnessing the contestants' courage and purpose, reminding me of the value of embracing one's strengths and advocating for others.


This experience influenced my perspective by showing me how beauty and intellect can work together to promote meaningful messages. I learned that a platform, no matter how big or small, can be used to inspire others and address important issues. I plan to apply this learning by supporting initiatives that align with my values and by encouraging others to express themselves confidently. If I could attend another Miss Silliman pageant, I would engage more deeply by learning about the contestants’ platforms beforehand and supporting their causes.


Because of this Miss Silliman experience, I am reminded of the power of self-expression and advocacy, and how each person can make a difference by embracing their unique strengths and using their voice for positive change.
